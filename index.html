IITB-PROC

Digital course project '19

We as a team of 4 people made a multi-cycle processor, IITB-Proc, which was capable of executing 14 instruction sets provided. Using VHDL as HDL , we implemented a 16-bit very simple computer. The IITB-Proc is an 8-register, 16-bit computer system which uses point-to point communication infrastructure.

IMPLEMENTATION OF A DIGITAL VOTING MACHINE

Feb'18-May'18

Consolidated the details of the operational principles of decoders and logic gates by implement-
ing it on a voting machine . Assembled a working circuit with D flip-flops, LM555 timer, decade counters, and 7 Segment
Display. Screened the vote count with 8 second time delay on two 7 segment displays.

PARTITIONING OF DIGITAL CIRCUITS FOR DISTRIBUTED SIMULATION

June'19-Present

Digital sequential circuit partitioning for logic simulation over a network of processing units while minimizing communication at the cost of some redundant computation Comparing approaches using sub-modular set function based greedy splitting.
